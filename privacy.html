Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Fortune Capital Group ("FCG", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Form Data: When you submit a contact form, we collect your name, email address, and any other information you provide.
  • Admin Data: If you use the admin dashboard, we collect your login credentials and any information you submit about closed deals, including uploaded images.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our website (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited) for analytics and security.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To manage and display closed deals on the website
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our website and admin system
  • To comply with legal obligations

How We Protect Your Information

  • All sensitive data (including admin credentials) is encrypted and stored securely.
  • Deal and contact data is stored in MongoDB Atlas (cloud database) and images are stored in Cloudinary (cloud image hosting).
  • Access to admin features is protected by secure authentication and authorization.

Third-Party Services

  • MongoDB Atlas: Used for secure cloud data storage. View MongoDB Atlas Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary: Used for secure image hosting. View Cloudinary Privacy Policy
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider. View Vercel Privacy Policy

Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the email below.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
